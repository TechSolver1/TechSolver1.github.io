What's a Technomancer?

A technomancer is a practitioner of technomancy:

technomancy (n.)

  1. A form of magical ability that either affects technology, is used through technology, or is gained through the use of technology.
  2. The blending of magic and technology.

 

Have you ever brought in a troubleshooter to diagnose an issue to find the issue nonexistent the entire time the troubleshooter is near?  If so, you've seen technomancy in action.
